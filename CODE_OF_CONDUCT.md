# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in ClearBill respectful, constructive, and welcoming for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Give feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Be careful with healthcare-related examples, billing data, and user privacy.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing secrets, tokens, real medical bills, patient records, or protected health information.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants whose behavior harms the project community.
